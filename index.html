New Era of Card Battles

CyberJungles

In a world where people used cybernetics to create perfect and
deadly animal-machines, a terrible event occurred. Control over
some of these creations was lost, and they began attacking
humans, destroying everything in their path. In this dystopia,
the surviving humans formed different factions and compete
with each other using their cyber beasts in a card battle. The
player must choose their faction and assemble a deck of cyber
animals for battle.

Characters

Healer

She can use the ability
"Restoration" to restore the health or of herself or allied creatures.

Hunter

He can place a "Trap" on the battlefield that deals damage to enemy creatures when they enter play or attack the Hunter.

Shadow

She can use "Dark Shield" which
protects her from magical attacks
and absorbs some of the damage

Pirate

He has the ability to "Steal", which
allows him to steal a card from the
opponent's deck or hand."

Arrow

She can use "Slow Arrow", which
slows down the movement of her

attacks.

Ghost

He can use "Phantom Curse" to
place a curse on the opponent's
